The Hard Math

ROAS is a lie.
Optimizing for it is bleeding your margin.

If your ad agency or tool brags about a "4.5x ROAS," they are hiding the truth. ROAS ignores your Cost of Goods Sold (COGS), warehouse costs, payment processor fees, and shipping costs. Scale a thin-margin campaign, and you aren't growing—you are losing money faster.

Omni OS Profit Formula (POAS)
POAS = (Net Revenue - COGS - Shipping) / Ad Spend

If POAS is below 1.0, you are losing cash on every conversion. Omni OS catches this instantly.
